Agni Club Register — Step-by-Step Walkthrough

A friendly, screenshot-free walkthrough of the Agni Club Register process, with a focus on the decisions that matter.

Before you tap Register

The Agni Club Register flow only takes two minutes, but the decisions you make in those two minutes matter for as long as you use the account. This walkthrough focuses on the decisions, not the taps.

Prerequisites

You are 18 or older. You have your own active mobile number. You have a comfortable place to sit for a few minutes. You have decided in advance whether and how much you are willing to deposit. If any of these is not true, this is a great moment to stop.

Step 1 — Open the official app

Open the Agni Club application that you installed from the operator's official source. Do not follow a signup link from a message; go to the app directly.

Step 2 — Tap Register

You will see a Register (or Sign up) button on the launch screen. Tap it. The registration form opens.

Step 3 — Enter your mobile number

Enter your own number, with the correct country code. Double-check every digit — the OTP will be sent here, and if you mistype, you will not receive it.

Step 4 — Verify with OTP

An SMS arrives with a 4–6 digit code. Enter it in the form. Do not tap 'Resend' repeatedly; wait the full 60 seconds first.

Step 5 — Choose a strong password

This is the most important decision in the flow. Use a password that is unique to this app. Twelve characters minimum. A mix of letters and numbers. Do not reuse your email or bank password.

Step 6 — Optional invitation code

If a friend has genuinely referred you, enter their code. If not, leave it blank. Do not enter codes from random public posts.

Step 7 — Set responsible-gaming limits

Before you leave the setup flow, configure any time and deposit limits the app offers. Thirty seconds now saves days of regret later.

After registration

Log in using the credentials you just set. Spend the first session watching, not staking. Read our Responsible Gaming guide before your first deposit.

Closing thought

The Agni Club Register flow is short and well-designed. The security and safety of your account are almost entirely a function of the decisions you make during those two minutes. Take them seriously, then relax and enjoy the app on your terms.
