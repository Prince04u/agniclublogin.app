Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what information Agni Club Guide collects when you visit our website, how we use it, and the choices available to you. We aim to collect as little as possible.

What we collect

We collect anonymised, aggregated analytics (page views, referring pages, approximate country, device type). If you submit the contact form or email us, we receive whatever you send us — typically a name, email address and message.

How we use it

Analytics help us understand which articles are most useful and where to focus improvements. Contact form messages are used only to reply to you.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not share personal data with third parties except when required by law. Analytics is processed by our privacy-respecting analytics provider on our behalf.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies and privacy-respecting analytics. We do not use advertising trackers or cross-site profiling cookies.

Your rights

You may request a copy of any personal data we hold about you, or ask us to delete it, by emailing hello@agniclublogin.app.

Security

The site is served over HTTPS and static content is delivered via a CDN. Contact form submissions are transmitted securely.

Children

The site is not directed at children under 18 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

Any material changes to this policy will be posted here with a revised effective date. Continued use after a change constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Questions about this policy: hello@agniclublogin.app.
