Agni Club Register — Step-by-Step Signup Walkthrough

A clear, independent explanation of the Agni Club Register screen — what fields to expect, what to double-check, and how to start your account on the right foot.

Before you register

Registration takes about two minutes, but the decisions you make in those two minutes affect the security of your account for as long as you use it. Before you tap 'Register', do three quick checks: you are downloading the app from the correct official source, you are 18 or older and legally allowed to use the app in your country, and you can afford to lose any amount you might later deposit. If any of these three are shaky, stop here — this is a great moment to close the app and reconsider.

Fields you'll see

A typical Agni Club Register screen asks for four things: your mobile number, a password, an OTP for verification, and an optional invitation code. That is it. Any signup form that additionally demands your bank details, ID upload, or email password up front is not the real service. The real service collects only what is strictly necessary to create the account.

OTP and verification

After you enter your mobile number, an OTP is sent by SMS. Enter it in the app. If it does not arrive within 60 seconds, tap 'Resend'. The OTP proves that you own the phone number and prevents someone else from registering an account with a number that is not theirs.

Invitation codes

An invitation code is a short string given by an existing user. It usually earns the inviter a small referral bonus. Entering an invitation code is optional. If a friend has genuinely referred you, enter theirs. Do not enter random codes copied from public forums — the code owner, not you, receives the benefit.

Choosing a password

This is the single most important decision in the whole flow. Use a password that is unique to this account. Do not reuse the password from your email, bank, or social media. Aim for at least twelve characters and a mix of letters and numbers. If you have a password manager, use it. If you don't, write the password down and store the paper somewhere private — a written password kept safely is stronger than a memorable weak one.

After you register

Once the account is created, take one minute to configure the basics before doing anything else. Set a daily play-time limit if the app offers one. Set a maximum daily deposit if you are considering deposits. Enable any two-step verification option. Only then, if you want, start exploring the app.

Common mistakes

The mistakes we see most often, in order: registering with a borrowed phone number, reusing a password from another account, entering a stranger's invitation code, and skipping any responsible-gaming setup. Each of these takes seconds to avoid and saves hours of regret later.

Final signup checklist

  1. You are 18+ and legally allowed to use the app.
  2. You are downloading from the official source.
  3. You are using your own active mobile number.
  4. Your password is unique and stored somewhere safe.
  5. You have set responsible-gaming limits before your first deposit.

The Agni Club Register flow is designed to be fast, and that speed is fine — as long as you have already made the important decisions before you start typing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Agni Club Signup free?

Creating an account is free. Any gameplay involving deposits carries financial risk.

Can I register with two mobile numbers?

One account per person is the standard rule. Multiple accounts risk being flagged and closed.

Do I need to enter an invitation code?

No. It is optional and only benefits the inviter.

What is the minimum age?

18. If you are under 18 you must not create an account.
